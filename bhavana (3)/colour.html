The color red is significant in many cultures. It is associated with love in Western culture, and it symbolizes good fortune and joy in Asian cultures.

The color blue represents both the sky and the sea and is associated with open spaces, freedom, intuition, imagination, inspiration, and sensitivity.

Voilet combines the calm stability of blue and the fierce energy of red. The color Voilet is often associated with royalty, nobility, luxury, power, and ambition.
